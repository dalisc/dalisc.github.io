My Projects

Hackathons

SpotNow (Hack.Asia 2019)

For the Daimler Empowering Mobility Challenge, my team and I pitched an idea for smartcars to help detect unreported road accidents. Our idea made it to the finals for which I prototyped a video analytics engine to detect vehicle accidents and plot accident hotspots on the map, using Django, Google Video Intelligence API, OpenWeatherMap API, and Mapbox. (Unfortunately, this repository was required to be private, but you can view our slide deck here!)

O.Life (Circles.Hack 2019)

This personalized recommendation engine was developed for a virtual hackathon organised by Circles.Life. It uses collaborative filtering to cluster similar users together and content-based filtering to recommend specific items to users in a cluster. We used various APIs to do semantic analysis on event and movie descriptions to see how closely they match users' existing tastes and preferences. We were very proud to win the Most Innovative Award and the Diversity & Inclusion Award.

PokkaPet (Hackomania 2019)

I will always remember this as the hackathon where I won a Google Home Mini! My team and I developed a digital pet-themed diet tracker app using Unity and various Rapid Rapid APIs. We couldn't believe it when we became the Rakuten Rapid API Challenge Winner! Click here to view our presentation slides and here to view our developer showcase on the Rakuten blog.

YouTube Cat is a Chrome plugin developed by me and my friend Arjavi as a 2019 summer project.
